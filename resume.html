About
Arnott Ferels with an MArch(Prof) from ITB, specializes in computational and generative design. His expertise covers urban simulation, agent-based modeling, behavior simulation, and TOD. At ITB, he contributed to research on computational tools, generative methods, and optimization for the built environment, focusing on CFD, spatial analysis, design modularization, and advanced modeling. By integrating cutting-edge technologies, he is committed to advancing architectural design and is eager to collaborate on innovative, sustainable solutions.
