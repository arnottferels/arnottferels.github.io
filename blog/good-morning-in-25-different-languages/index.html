Good Morning in 25 Different Languages

Jul 2024
Image: Renata Daniel
good morninglanguageworld
Cite

Good morning is a universal greeting that transcends borders and cultures, yet it takes on unique expressions in different languages around the globe. From Asia to Europe, and from the Americas to Oceania, this phrase is a simple yet powerful way to start the day with a positive interaction. Below is a table showcasing how to say ‘Good Morning’ in 25 different languages, along with the countries where these languages are spoken.

Asia#

Chinese (Mandarin)
Phrase: 早上好 (Zǎo shàng hǎo)
Countries: China 1, Taiwan 2, Singapore 3

Japanese
Phrase: おはようございます (Ohayō gozaimasu)
Countries: Japan 4

Korean
Phrase: 좋은 아침입니다 (Joheun achimimnida)
Countries: South Korea 5

Hindi
Phrase: सुप्रभात (Suprabhat)
Countries: India 6

Bengali
Phrase: সুপ্রভাত (Suprabhat)
Countries: Bangladesh 7, India 6

Thai
Phrase: สวัสดีตอนเช้า (Sawasdee ton chao)
Countries: Thailand 8

Vietnamese
Phrase: Chào buổi sáng
Countries: Vietnam 9

Persian
Phrase: صبح بخیر (Sobh bekheir)
Countries: Iran 10

Arabic
Phrase: صباح الخير (Sabah al-khair)
Countries: Saudi Arabia 11, United Arab Emirates 12, Egypt 13, etc.

Hebrew
Phrase: בוקר טוב (Boker tov)
Countries: Israel 14

Europe#

English
Phrase: Good Morning
Countries: United Kingdom 15, Ireland 16, Malta 17, etc.

Spanish
Phrase: Buenos Días
Countries: Spain 18, most of Latin America

French
Phrase: Bonjour
Countries: France 19, Belgium 20, Switzerland 21, Canada 22, etc.

German
Phrase: Guten Morgen
Countries: Germany 23, Austria 24, Switzerland 21

Italian
Phrase: Buongiorno
Countries: Italy 25

Portuguese
Phrase: Bom Dia
Countries: Portugal 26, Brazil 27

Russian
Phrase: Доброе утро (Dobroye utro)
Countries: Russia 28, Belarus 29, Kazakhstan 30

Dutch
Phrase: Goedemorgen
Countries: Netherlands 31, Belgium 20

Greek
Phrase: Καλημέρα (Kaliméra)
Countries: Greece 32

Swedish
Phrase: God morgon
Countries: Sweden 33

Norwegian
Phrase: God morgen
Countries: Norway 34

Danish
Phrase: God morgen
Countries: Denmark 35

Finnish
Phrase: Hyvää huomenta
Countries: Finland 36

North America#

English
Phrase: Good Morning
Countries: United States 37, Canada 22

Spanish
Phrase: Buenos Días
Countries: Mexico 38, Central America, Caribbean

South America#

Spanish
Phrase: Buenos Días
Countries: Argentina 39, Colombia 40, Chile 41, etc.

Portuguese
Phrase: Bom Dia
Countries: Brazil 27

Oceania#

English
Phrase: Good Morning
Countries: Australia 42, New Zealand 43
