TLDR — I was honored as one of the outstanding postgraduate students during SAPPD's 2022 welcome event at ITB's East Hall. As part of the ITB 2022 New Student Admission program, SAPPD held a welcome event for new students for the Undergraduate and Postgraduate Programs at the East Hall of ITB on August 16 and August 18, 2022, respectively.

This event marked the first time the program returned to an in-perspectiveon format since 2019 and was attended by all new students, the Dean’s Office, Program Study Chairs, Heads of Expertise Groups, and Academic Advisors.

In 2022, SAPPD welcomed a total of 252 undergraduate students, including 19 from the International Pathway (Architecture and Regional and City Planning), and 166 postgraduate students.

During the event, the SAPPD Dean’s Office introduced SAPPK, including its Vision and Mission, academic programs, student-related procedures, and introduced the Program Study Chairs, Heads of Expertise Groups, academic advisors, and coordinators.

Additionally, SAPPD took the opportunity to honor outstanding students from both the undergraduate and postgraduate levels with the SAPPD Dean’s List 2022 awards.
