Title         : Indonesian Architecture Students Provide Public Space Solutions with Bamboo Touches

Abstract      : I and other Indonesian architecture students gathered at UMN for the TKI-MAI 33 Urban Intervention Workshop, focusing on bamboo installations to enhance public spaces. The event aimed to foster creativity and sustainability in architecture, showcasing innovative uses of bamboo for ecological building solutions.
