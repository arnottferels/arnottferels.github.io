Abstract      : At the SAPPD April 2023 Graduation Celebration, held at ITB's Lapangan Cinta, I had the honor to represent the master's architecture graduates. The event, which included 144 graduates across various programs, featured heartfelt congratulations from Dean Sri Maryati and messages from Vice Dean Aswin Indraprastha, Ph.D. It concluded with a group photo session and farewell remarks, embodying a joyous and memorable occasion for all participants.
