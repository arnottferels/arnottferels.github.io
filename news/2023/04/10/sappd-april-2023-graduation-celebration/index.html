SAPPD April 2023 Graduation Celebration

2023-04-10

TL;DR — At the SAPPD April 2023 Graduation Celebration, held at ITB's Lapangan Cinta, I had the honor to represent the master's architecture graduates. The event, which included 144 graduates across various programs, featured heartfelt congratulations from Dean Sri Maryati and messages from Vice Dean Aswin Indraprastha, Ph.D. It concluded with a group photo session and farewell remarks, embodying a joyous and memorable occasion for all participants.

On Saturday, April 8, 2023, SAPPD held a Graduation Celebration event at Lapangan Cinta, beside the East CC Building, ITB. The event took place from 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

The event began with the ITB Open Session procession at Sabuga at 8:30 AM. Parents of the graduates were provided with waiting room facilities inside Labtek IXA and IXB Buildings.

After conducting the Open Session agenda, the graduates were escorted and paraded by the committee members from HMP and SAPPK staff from Sabuga to Lapangan Cinta ITB.

The participating graduates looked happy and joyful, maintaining their spirits even during fasting.

Once the participants had gathered at Lapangan Cinta, the event was opened by the MC led by SAPPD lecturers, Mrs. Maya and Mrs. Medria, for the SAPPD Graduation Celebration.

The event was welcomed by the Dean of SAPPD , Dr. Sri Maryati, who congratulated the graduates. After that, the event continued with the singing of the national anthem, Indonesia Raya. The next segment was led by the Vice Dean for Academic Affairs, Aswin Indraprastha, Ph.D., who shared Messages and Impressions from the representatives of the graduates.

The event proceeded with the reading of the names of SAPPD graduates by Mrs. Rina and Mrs. Winda from the SAPPD staff.

The total number of SAPPD graduates is as follows: 144 Graduates, including:

  • Undergraduate Program: 29 Graduates
  • Master’s Program: 106 Graduates
  • Doctoral Program: 5 Graduates
  • PSPPI Program: 4 Graduates.

After the reading of the names of the graduates, the next segment was a group photo session with the dean’s office and program chairs.

Following that, the event continued with farewell remarks from the representative of the master’s architecture graduates, Arnott Ferels, and his parents.

The event concluded with a closing prayer led by Dr. Mohammad Zaini Dahlan.
