Title         : 98 Architectural Works was Exhibited at ATRIA Architectural Design Exhibition
Keywords      : ATRIA, Architectural design exhibition, NURTURE
Authors       : Adi Permana

Publisher     : Bandung Institute of Technology
Abstract      : Our project named NURTURE was exhibited at the ATRIA Architectural Design Exhibition 2023 hosted by ITB's School of Architecture. The exhibition featured 98 works under the theme 'Designing to Contribute,' focusing on social, energy, and natural resource issues. NURTURE contributes to advancing architectural discourse and addressing contemporary challenges like climate change and urban transformation, curated by industry leaders to engage both professionals and the public in architectural innovation and education.
