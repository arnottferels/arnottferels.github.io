Hello!

I’m Arnott Ferels.

With a passion for computational design and a background in architecture, I enjoy exploring new ways to tackle design challenges. It is particularly fulfilling when these challenges intersect with ideas about urban sustainability, human behavior, and modular systems.

Recently, I’ve been focusing on AI, machine learning, and data-driven techniques And implementation to create sustainable design solutions. These methods enhance work in optimization algorithms, generative design, simulation-based design, urban simulation, and BIM. The tools I use most are Grasshopper and Revit. You can find other tools I use here.

Feel free to connect or collaborate.
